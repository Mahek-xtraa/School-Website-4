Hiranandani Hospital

Estabilished in February 2004

Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital is a Public Charitable Trust Hospital, founded in honour of the legend, Padma Bhushan Dr L H Hiranandani, came into existence in February 2004.

The challenge of the early days was enormous, to foster the fledgling hospital into an enduring symbol of healthcare in the country. theyrecognised early on, that to make a real and substantial contribution, one had to redefine the standards themselves, instead of doing more of the same. That set the tone of our endeavour and guided us every step of the way, luring us towards ambitious solutions, services, teamwork and quality that suited us.

The results were quick to show. In 2007 they became the first hospital in Western India to receive the prestigious NABH Accreditation for Hospitals and Healthcare providers, an honour accorded to only eight hospitals in the country at the time. The IMC Ramakrishna Bajaj National Quality Award trophy, for excellence in healthcare, followed in 2008. In 2009, they were the proud recipients of the coveted International Asia Pacific Quality Award (The Malcolm Baldridge Award) as it is known outside in the United States.

  • Temerature Checking

    There are guards who check the kids temperature before entering the school and before exiting as due to Covid Reasons no parent would like to take risks. By the end of every week the kids reports reach the school portal. If any suspicious activity is seen.. a message is sent to the respective parent for not sending their kid for 3 days. On the 4th Day if the temperature is not decreased or has increased then the kid must do a Covid test. If the test is positive, the shcool remains closed for 3 weeks and continues with online school. During that week if any kid gets any symptoms they must immediately do a Coronavirus Test ASAP.
